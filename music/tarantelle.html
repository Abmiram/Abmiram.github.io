Excerpts from Grande Tarantelle

By Louis Moreau Gottschalk

Arranged by Ross Birdsall


For five players on two 4.3 octave (low A) marimbas. This piece is a fun, fast-paced journey exploring ideas in 6/8 time with a few quick position changes to keep your performers and audience on their toes.

Originally premiered by the Coleman middle school percussion ensemble at the 2019 University of South Florida Marimba Festival, this piece is perfect for an advanced middle school ensemble or an early high school group.

Number of performers: Five

Difficulty: Early intermediate

Instrumentation: Two marimbas (4.3 octave)
